Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Subjects, Semester-wise Syllabus, Top Exams

Overview

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML) courses provide students with an in-depth understanding of algorithms, neural networks, deep learning, and data-driven decision-making. The AIML syllabus covers a blend of mathematics, statistics, programming, and cutting-edge AI techniques used in various industries such as healthcare, finance, robotics, and automation. AIML falls under the broader domain of Computer Science and Engineering, equipping students with expertise in designing and implementing intelligent systems. Some important AIML subjects include Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Reinforcement Learning, Computer Vision, Big Data Analytics, Neural Networks, and AI Ethics.
